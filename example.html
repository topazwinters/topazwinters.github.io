t.w. / an inhale

about

my name is topaz. i am a poet & multidisciplinary artist.

since 2016 i have been recording every piece of media that i consume. this is a space for readers to see the art that inspires my own, & a space for me to reflect on how my consumption of media has evolved throughout my personal history.

each year in media is listed in reverse chronological order.

if you would like to see my thoughts on or ratings of anything below, or chat about media with me, i'll direct you to my goodreads, letterboxd, spotify, & annual love lists.

my own work in poetry, film, music, & digital art can be found at my website.
